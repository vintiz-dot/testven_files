The JIS curriculum values "Practice" (*Hành*) and "Aesthetics" (*Mỹ*). This directly addresses the school’s need for an educator who can bridge ESL and Art within the Cambridge Primary framework.

2. Alignment with Japanese Educational Values:
I understand that in the Japanese educational model, the classroom is a community founded on respect and discipline. My teaching philosophy emphasizes collaborative learning and shared responsibility. I view the teacher not just as an instructor, but as a moral role model who guides students in etiquette and resilience alongside grammar and phonics. I am eager to support the school’s unique culture, including the emphasis on physical health and manners.

At Victoria Thăng Long and Happy English Club, I have extensively utilized Inquiry-Based Learning to make the Cambridge curriculum accessible to Vietnamese learners. I have a proven track record of differentiating instruction for mixed-ability classes, using visual scaffolding and kinesthetic activities to ensure that every child, regardless of their starting point, can engage with the material and build confidence.
