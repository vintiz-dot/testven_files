These recommendations are drawn directly from administrator free-response answers on the Module 41 survey and from teacher self-identified coaching requests. Each coaching plan names a coaching model, a measurable starting goal, and a five-meeting arc (exceeding the template's minimum of three) across an 8-week coaching cycle.

  • Focus: Data-driven instruction / moving from dashboard to grouping
  • Goal: Implement weekly data-driven small groups based on IXL diagnostic tools.

Section C · Alignment & Evaluation Plan

All PD and coaching proposals align with the school's vision of turning technology from a passive consumption tool into an active, student-centered learning mechanism.

Evaluation: Success will be measured via post-PD surveys, subsequent classroom walkthrough data on student engagement, and tracking the frequency of technology-facilitated differentiation.
