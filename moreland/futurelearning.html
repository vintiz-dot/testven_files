Classroom Exemplar: The Logic Lab

Learning by Doing: Experiential Science and Math

In the Logic Lab, students move away from passive listening and engage in Project-Based Learning (PBL) within a Makerspace environment. They build physical models that connect directly to unit topics, experiencing science and math in a concrete, hands-on way.

The learning process focuses on iterative design and corrective feedback loops. For example, when building an elevator model, students discover the principles of electric motors, gravity, weights, and counterweights. When a design fails, they learn to troubleshoot and iterate—teaching them resilience and that failure is an essential step toward understanding and mastery.
